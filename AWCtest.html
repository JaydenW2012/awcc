How To Play

古代战棋融合了中国象棋与战略战棋元素。

  • 兵 / 卒 (Pawn): Moves 1 step forward. After crossing the river, forward, left, or right (not backward).
  • 剑 (Arrow): Orthogonal any distance; capture by jumping exactly 1 piece.
  • 军 (Soldier): Up to 5 steps orthogonally.
  • 炮 / 砲 (Cannon): Orthogonal; capture by jumping 1 or 2 pieces.
  • 車 / 俥 (Chariot): Rook-like orthogonal slides.
  • 马 / 傌 (Horse): L-shape or 2-step straight.
  • 相 / 象 (Elephant): 2 diagonal (田); blocked at center; 2 straight with empty midpoint.
  • 虎 / 狮 (Tiger / Lion): 1–2 steps within a 2×2 area.
  • 仕 / 士 (Advisor): Any distance diagonal or orthogonal; clear path.
  • 将 / 帥 (King): 1 step any direction. Capture the enemy king to win.
